About Us


Background

Azrul Afifi & Azuan was founded in 2006 by a group of energetic young lawyers eager to apply a fresh approach to practicing law. Despite the firm’s relatively young age, we are very proud and privileged to have been associated with and to have the trust of many big corporations and institutions. We believe this is down to our personal and client-centered approach to providing legal services. It’s what makes us different. Understanding our clients, the business and legal challenges they face makes us an asset to them.


Our Commitment

We work hard to build a small yet solid and dynamic group of young professionals. We also take great pride in striving to deliver the high quality works and services on every matter entrusted to us. Our clients have our promise for creative and practical solutions to each of their unique needs. At Azrul Afifi & Azuan, we endeavor as a firm to be responsive to our clients’ expectations within the ethical bounds and in manners consistent with the highest professional ideals. We trust that our breadth of expertise together with commitment to excellence will enable us to effectively address our clients’ diverse business and legal challenges.
